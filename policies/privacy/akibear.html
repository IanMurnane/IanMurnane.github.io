Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 19 August 2024

Welcome to Aki Bear AI by AppArcade Studios. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. App Description

Aki Bear AI uses artificial intelligence to generate and read user stories, providing a creative and engaging experience.

2. Information Collection

We collect minimal personal information necessary for user login, such as your email address or Google account profile.

3. Non-Personal Information Collection

We collect non-personal information such as error logs, app usage data, and content requests to help us improve our services.

4. Purpose of Data Collection

The information we collect is used to enhance our services and ensure reliability.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Social Login Data: Handled by the respective provider (e.g., Google).
  • Error and Usage Data: Securely managed by AWS (Amazon Web Services).

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third parties. Non-personal, anonymized data may be used for internal analysis and to improve the app.

7. Secure Data Handling

All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols. Access to personal data is limited to authorized personnel only.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting us through the provided channels. Once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered.

9. User Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you and to request its correction or deletion. Contact us using the details below to exercise these rights.

10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies or other tracking technologies. The only tracking data collected is the anonymous IP address used for server communication.

11. Children’s Privacy

Aki Bear AI does not collect information from users other than what is necessary for login services. We do not specifically target or collect data from children under 13.

12. Changes to the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary. The latest version will always be available here.
