Privacy Policy - Secure Player

Last Updated: June 21, 2026

Welcome to Secure Player. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Secure Player operates, what information may be processed while using the app, and how integrated third-party services are used.

1. App Purpose and Privacy Approach

Secure Player is designed to provide local image, video and audio playback with a privacy-conscious approach.

The app focuses on core media functionality and aims to minimise unnecessary data collection and online activity while maintaining reliability and supporting ongoing development.

2. Information Processed by the App

Media Files

Media files selected within Secure Player are processed locally on your device for playback and related media features.

The developer does not collect, upload, analyse, or store the contents of videos, images, audio files, playback history, or viewing activity.

Operational Diagnostics and Reliability Information

Secure Player may collect limited operational and diagnostic information to help maintain reliability, improve performance, and identify technical issues.

Examples may include:

  • App launches and startup events
  • Error reports and diagnostic information
  • Technical logs and stack traces generated during failures
  • Device model and operating system version
  • Performance and stability indicators

This information is used to understand application behaviour, diagnose faults, and improve app quality. It is not used to analyse or collect media content played within the app.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

Secure Player displays advertisements to support ongoing development. Advertisements may be provided through third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob.

Advertising services may process information in accordance with their own privacy practices and applicable laws. This may include:

  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Operating system version
  • Advertising identifiers where available
  • Consent preferences

Where required by law, users may be presented with a consent choice before personalised advertising is enabled.

Use of advertising-related information is governed by the applicable third-party provider privacy policies.

4. Media Processing Components

Secure Player includes embedded open-source media processing libraries, including FFmpeg, to provide audio and video functionality. These components operate locally on your device and do not transmit media content to the developer.

5. Children’s Privacy

Secure Player is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe information has been provided unintentionally, please contact us.

6. Data Security and Retention

Diagnostic information may be retained for a limited period to support reliability, maintenance, and issue resolution. We take reasonable measures to minimise unnecessary data collection and protect application integrity.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect operational, legal, or product changes. The latest version will always be published with an updated revision date.
