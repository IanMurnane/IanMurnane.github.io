Privacy Policy - One Line Logger

Last Updated: April 22, 2026

Welcome to One Line Logger. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and handled when you use the app and its associated services.

1. Overview of the Service

One Line Logger provides a user interface for viewing and managing application logs that are sent to and stored in the cloud.

A separate helper plugin (for example, for Flutter applications) allows developers to send log data from their applications to cloud infrastructure (such as AWS), which can then be viewed and managed within the app.

2. Data Collection and Use

Log Data Submitted by Users

The app processes log data that is explicitly sent by you through your applications using the logging plugin. This data may include:

  • Application logs (messages, errors, debug information)
  • Timestamps and log levels
  • Application identifiers (App IDs)

This data is:

  • Stored in cloud infrastructure
  • Displayed within the app interface for monitoring and debugging
  • Fully controlled by you as the developer/user

Important: You are responsible for ensuring that any log data you send does not contain sensitive personal data unless you have the legal right to process it.

Account and Authentication Data

If authentication is used, basic account-related data may be processed, such as:

  • Email address
  • User ID

This data is used solely for authentication, account management, and access control.

3. Subscription and Payments

The app offers optional paid features through subscription services managed by third-party providers (such as RevenueCat).

These features may include:

  • Managing multiple App IDs
  • Marking log data as private
  • Receiving error and debug notifications

Subscription handling (including payments, billing, and receipts) is processed by the respective platform (for example, Apple App Store or Google Play) and/or the subscription provider. We do not store your payment information.

4. Data Storage and Security

Log data is stored using cloud services (such as AWS infrastructure). By default, all log data is public and can be retrieved by anyone entering a valid App ID. During an active subscription, logs may be set to private mode. In this mode, reasonable technical and organizational measures are taken to protect data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Data visibility depends on your configuration:

  • By default, logs are accessible with a valid App ID
  • Pro features may allow logs to be marked as private

5. Notifications

If enabled, the app may send notifications related to log events, such as errors or debug messages. These notifications are based solely on the log data you provide and your configured preferences.

6. Third-Party Services

The app may rely on third-party services for functionality, including:

  • Cloud hosting and storage (such as AWS)
  • Subscription management (such as RevenueCat)
  • Authentication providers

These services may process limited technical data (such as IP address or device information) as required to provide their functionality. Their data handling is governed by their respective privacy policies.

7. Cookies and Tracking

The app does not use cookies or tracking technologies for advertising purposes.

However, platform providers or integrated services may use minimal technical identifiers required for authentication, security, or subscription validation.

8. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Data Retention

Log data is retained for as long as necessary to provide the service. You may delete log data or stop sending logs at any time.

Account-related data is retained as long as your account is active.

10. Your Responsibilities

As a user of the logging system, you are responsible for:

  • The content of the logs you send
  • Ensuring compliance with applicable data protection laws (including GDPR)
  • Avoiding inclusion of sensitive personal data unless properly handled

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
