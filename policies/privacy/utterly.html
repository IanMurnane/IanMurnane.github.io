Privacy Policy - Utterly

Last Updated: July 6, 2026

Welcome to Utterly. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Utterly operates, what information may be processed while using the app, and how integrated third-party services are used to power our language learning features.

1. App Purpose and Privacy Approach

Utterly is a language learning application designed to help users practice new languages through simulated conversations and interactive AI-driven chats.

The app focuses on providing an immersive learning experience while minimising unnecessary data collection. We use anonymous analytics to improve the app and rely on secure third-party APIs to power the conversational AI.

2. Information Processed by the App

Simulated Chats and AI Processing

To provide dynamic language practice, Utterly processes the text and inputs you provide during simulated chats. This conversational data is sent to a third-party AI provider (Google's Gemini API) to generate context-aware responses in your target language.

Please Note: We strongly advise against sharing personal, sensitive, or identifiable information (such as your full name, address, or financial details) during your simulated chats.

Anonymous Analytics and Diagnostics

Utterly collects limited, anonymous usage data to help us understand how the app is used, improve performance, and identify technical issues. We use One Line Logger for this purpose.

Examples of collected data may include:

  • App launches and session durations
  • Feature usage frequency (e.g., how often specific chat scenarios are used)
  • Error reports and technical logs generated during failures
  • Device model and operating system version

This information is completely anonymous and cannot be tied back to your individual identity. It is used strictly to improve the learning experience and app stability.

3. Third-Party Services

Utterly integrates specific third-party services to function effectively:

  • Google Gemini API: Powers the core conversational AI and language processing features. The data sent to this API is governed by Google's API terms of service and privacy policies.
  • One Line Logger: Used to capture anonymous analytics and diagnostic information to help us maintain and improve the application.

4. Children’s Privacy

Utterly is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe information has been provided unintentionally, please contact us.

5. Data Security and Retention

Chat data is processed in real-time to generate AI responses and is not stored on our personal servers. Anonymous diagnostic information may be retained for a limited period to support reliability, maintenance, and issue resolution. We take reasonable measures to protect application integrity and user privacy.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect operational, legal, or product changes. The latest version will always be published with an updated revision date.
