Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 9 May 2025

Welcome to Story Bear AI by AppArcade Studios. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. App Description

Story Bear AI uses artificial intelligence to generate and read user stories, providing a creative and engaging experience.

2. Information Collection

We collect minimal personal information necessary for user login, such as your email address or Google account profile.

3. Non-Personal Information Collection

We collect non-personal information such as error logs, app usage data, and content requests to help us improve our services.

4. Purpose of Data Collection

The information we collect is used to enhance our services, improve performance, and ensure reliability.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Social Login Data: Handled by the respective provider (e.g., Google).
  • Error and Usage Data: Securely managed by AWS (Amazon Web Services).

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third parties. Non-personal, anonymized data may be used for internal analysis and to improve the app.

7. Secure Data Handling

All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols. Access to personal data is limited to authorized personnel only.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can request the deletion of your data at any time through the app settings (if available) or by contacting us at support@apparcadestudios.com. Once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. We will complete the deletion process within 30 days of receiving your request.

9. User Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal data we hold about you. You can also request a copy of your data before deletion. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email provided below.

10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies or other tracking technologies. The only tracking data collected is the anonymous IP address used for server communication.

11. Children’s Privacy

Story Bear AI does not knowingly collect information from users under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us, and we will promptly delete it.

12. Changes to the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The latest version will always be available here. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed of any changes.
