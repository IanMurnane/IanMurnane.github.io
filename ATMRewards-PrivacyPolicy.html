ATM Rewards allows users to earn rewards through various activities while keeping track of their account balance.

We do not collect any personal information beyond what is necessary for app functionality. The only user data collected is a Google login to maintain user accounts and their balance.

The Google login is used solely to associate user accounts with their balances and ensure secure access.

Our app may display advertisements to support its operation. Third-party ad networks may collect anonymized usage data for ad targeting purposes.

We do not share any user data with third parties. The Google login is used exclusively within the app for authentication and balance tracking.

6. Secure Data Handling

We prioritize security and do not store sensitive personal information. Your account balance is linked securely to your Google account.
